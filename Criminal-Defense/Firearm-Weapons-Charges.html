Defending Against Firearm Charges

Are you facing weapons charges for possessing a firearm? In New York City there is almost no licensing of people to carry firearms. Most likely, if the police stop you and you have a gun, you will be facing charges whether you used that gun in the commission of a crime or not.

Gun crimes in New York fall into a small number of categories, including:

  • Criminal use of a firearm — This charge involves committing another crime while possessing or displaying some sort of firearm.
  • Criminal possession of a weapon — This can be charged as a misdemeanor or felony depending on the facts of the situation. It can carry a maximum prison sentence of up to 25 years.

Licensed In Your State, Illegal In New York

Many people do not realize that the permit they have to carry a gun in their home state does not allow them to possess a firearm in New York City. Whether it is at the airport or on the highway passing through the state, there are many ways that a person can wind up on the wrong side of the law when it comes to firearms.
